# Privacy Policy for BeanBench

**Last updated: January 20, 2026**

BeanBench ("we", "our", or "us") is a specialty coffee logging and discovery app. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

## Summary

- **Local Mode**: Your data stays on your device. We don't collect anything.
- **Account Features**: If you create an account for community features, we collect only what's necessary.
- **We never sell your personal data.**

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Local Data (No Account Required)

When you use BeanBench without an account, all your data is stored locally on your device:

- Brew logs, coffee beans, roasters, and grinder settings
- App preferences and settings
- Recipe selections and timer history

**This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly enable iCloud Sync or create an account.**

### 1.2 iCloud Sync

If you enable iCloud Sync, your data is stored in your personal iCloud account. This data is governed by [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/). We do not have access to your iCloud data.

### 1.3 Account Data (Optional Community Features)

If you create an account to use community features (such as global ratings, reviews, or recommendations), we collect:

- Email address
- Display name (can be anonymous)
- Profile information you choose to provide
- Your public ratings and reviews
- Usage data to improve recommendations

### 1.4 Automatically Collected Data

When using online features, we may collect:

- Device type and operating system version
- App version
- Anonymous usage analytics (feature usage, crash reports)
- IP address (for security and regional features)

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- Provide and improve the BeanBench app
- Sync your data across your devices (if enabled)
- Display community ratings and reviews (if you participate)
- Provide personalized coffee recommendations (if enabled)
- Send important service updates (account-related only)
- Detect and prevent fraud or abuse
- Comply with legal obligations

**We do not use your data for advertising profiles or sell it to third parties.**

---

## 3. Community Features & Public Information

If you choose to participate in community features:

- **Ratings and reviews** you submit may be visible to other users
- Your **display name** will be shown with your public contributions
- You can use an anonymous display name
- You can delete your public contributions at any time

---

## 4. Personalized Recommendations

If you opt in to personalized recommendations:

- We analyze your brew history and ratings to suggest coffees you might enjoy
- This processing may occur on our servers or on your device
- You can disable personalized recommendations at any time in Settings
- Disabling will delete your recommendation profile from our servers

---

## 5. Third-Party Services

### 5.1 Analytics

We may use privacy-focused analytics services to understand how users interact with the app. These services collect anonymous, aggregated data and do not track you across other apps or websites.

### 5.2 Affiliate Partners

We may earn commissions when you purchase coffee or equipment through links in the app. When you click an affiliate link:

- The retailer may know you came from BeanBench
- We may receive information about completed purchases (not your personal details)
- We do not share your personal information with affiliate partners

### 5.3 Payment Processing

If we offer premium features, payments are processed by third-party providers (such as Apple's App Store). We do not store your payment information.

---

## 6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

- Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
- Encryption at rest for stored data
- Regular security assessments
- Limited employee access to user data

---

## 7. Data Retention

- **Local data**: Stays on your device until you delete it
- **Account data**: Retained while your account is active
- **Deleted accounts**: Data is deleted within 30 days
- **Anonymous analytics**: Retained in aggregated form indefinitely

---

## 8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

- **Access** your personal data
- **Correct** inaccurate data
- **Delete** your data ("right to be forgotten")
- **Export** your data in a portable format
- **Opt out** of personalized recommendations
- **Withdraw consent** for optional data processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at jah70@vt.edu.

### For California Residents (CCPA)

You have the right to know what personal information we collect and to request deletion. We do not sell personal information.

### For European Residents (GDPR)

Our legal basis for processing is:
- **Consent** for optional features like recommendations
- **Contract** for providing the service you requested
- **Legitimate interest** for security and fraud prevention

---

## 9. Children's Privacy

BeanBench is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us.
