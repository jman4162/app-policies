# Privacy Policy for Neon Drop

**Last updated:** January 28, 2026

## Summary

Neon Drop does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Your privacy is fully protected.

## Introduction

Neon Drop ("we", "our", or "the app") is a four-in-a-row strategy game developed by John Hodge. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect any personal data.** Neon Drop is designed with privacy in mind:

- No account creation required
- No personal information collected
- No analytics or tracking
- No location data collected
- No camera or microphone access
- No contact list access

## Local Data Storage

The app stores the following data locally on your device only:

- Game settings (sound, haptics, theme preferences)
- Total win count (for theme unlocking)
- Onboarding completion status

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

## Third-Party Services

Neon Drop does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. Your gameplay is completely private.

## In-App Purchases

If you make in-app purchases, the transaction is handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information. Please refer to [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/) for information about how Apple handles payment data.

## Children's Privacy

Neon Drop is rated 4+ and is safe for all ages. Since we do not collect any personal data, children can use the app without any privacy concerns.

## Data Security

Since we do not collect or transmit any personal data, there is no risk of your information being compromised through our app.

## Your Rights

As we do not collect any personal data, there is no personal information to access, modify, or delete. All game data stored locally on your device can be removed by uninstalling the app.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
